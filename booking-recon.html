Flags orphan rows on those boards too — rows that exist without a matching Arrivals entry, suggesting a cancelled or moved booking the watcher missed.
Architecture (post-2026-05-04 refactor): Guesty → Arrivals (authoritative, written by Make) → Cleans / Hot Tub Cleans (derived on demand by rota-planner and hot-tub-rota when a manager opens them and saves).
