🧹 Clean up Cleans rows

Find and delete phantom, duplicate and orphan rows on the Cleans board
Rota planner ‹ Hub
Three buckets:
• Phantoms — Cleans rows with no Arrivals link, no cleaner assigned, no notes, no completed date. Almost certainly auto-created and unwanted.
• Duplicates — multiple rows for the same booking (same arrivalId) OR same property+day. Keep one, delete the rest.
• Orphans — arrivalId set but the linked Arrivals row no longer exists (cancelled bookings the watcher didn't tidy up).

Completed cleans are never touched — we don't rewrite history. Safe to re-run after deletes; the page will reload and show what's left.
