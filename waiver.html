Terms & Conditions of Stay

1. Use of the Property

I/we agree to treat the property and its contents with care and respect. I/we understand that we are responsible for any damage caused by members of our party during the stay beyond normal fair wear and tear. Any damage must be reported to Be Here immediately.

2. Hot Tub Use

Where the property includes a hot tub, I/we acknowledge and accept the following:

  • The hot tub must not be used under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or medications that affect alertness or blood pressure.
  • Children under the age of 5 must not use the hot tub. Children under 16 must be supervised by an adult at all times.
  • Persons with heart conditions, high or low blood pressure, circulatory conditions, skin conditions, or who are pregnant should consult a doctor before using the hot tub.
  • Maximum recommended soak time is 15 minutes. Users should stay hydrated and exit the tub if they feel unwell.
  • No glass, food, or sharp objects in or around the hot tub. Swimwear must be worn at all times.
  • The hot tub cover must be replaced after each use.
  • We accept no liability for any illness, injury, or adverse reaction arising from use of the hot tub.

I/we accept full responsibility for the supervision of all children in our party at all times, including around the hot tub, any open water, and all outdoor areas. Be Here accepts no liability for accidents involving unsupervised children.

The Cotswold Water Park contains lakes, rivers, and open water areas in and around the estate. I/we understand and accept the associated risks, and accept that Be Here cannot be held responsible for accidents or injury occurring outside the immediate property boundary.

5. Liability

Be Here and its staff accept no liability for personal injury, loss, or damage to personal belongings during your stay, except where such liability cannot be excluded by law. Guests are encouraged to ensure they hold appropriate personal travel and liability insurance.

6. Property Rules

  • No smoking inside the property at any time.
  • No pets unless specifically agreed in writing prior to booking.
  • Guest numbers must not exceed the stated maximum occupancy for the property.
  • Noise levels must be kept reasonable, particularly between 10pm and 8am, out of consideration for neighbouring properties.
  • No events, parties, or gatherings beyond the booked guest party without prior written consent.
